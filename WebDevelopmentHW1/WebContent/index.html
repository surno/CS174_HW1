The Department of Computer Science strives to provide the highest quality education in
Computer Science at the undergraduate and graduate levels. We offer programs that lead to
the Bachelor of Science degree (BSCS), and the Master of Science degree (MSCS). Our
Bachelor's degree inComputer Science is accredited by the Computing Accreditation
Commission of ABET. Both programs, BSCS and MSCS, are accredited by the Western
Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC).
