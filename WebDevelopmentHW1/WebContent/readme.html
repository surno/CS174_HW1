I felt that some of the groups that the links were in for the original CS
department website were poorly organized. An example of this would be the
resources tab. This tab was basically used as a misc section and items that
they didn't feel belonged under the more specific drop downs, belonged there
I also felt that the separation of the advising tag and the academic programs tab
was unnecessary and that those sections can be easily combined.

What I did to change the way the is combine the advising and academic programs page
into one advising page. This made it into one page because all items that had to do
with advising is in one section. In the advising page, I also created another hierarchy
that divided the advising forms, majors, and the CS course information. For the next tab,
I combined the People and Contact tab from the original site and put them under a link
called faculty. For the last item in the list, I just kept resources very similar but
called it organizations and careers and left it that way.
